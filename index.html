My Story

I grew up in Livermore, California with my brother in a household that valued activity, curiosity, and family traditions like going to church every Sunday. As a kid I was always building or exploring something. I spent hours playing with K'NEX and Legos, riding bikes around the city, and getting lost in games like RuneScape.

Sports and outdoor activities were also a big part of growing up. I played baseball and hockey, and spent time paintballing and playing airsoft with friends. Looking back, I’ve always enjoyed environments where I could be active, competitive, and learning something new.

During high school my interests started to shift toward self-improvement and creativity. I became very dedicated to training in the gym and at the same time discovered music production. I became fascinated with hip hop and electronic music and spent countless hours learning how songs were made and experimenting with production.

Around that time I also became interested in business and personal development. I started reading books like Think and Grow Rich and How to Win Friends and Influence People, which sparked a curiosity about entrepreneurship, sales, and self education.

After high school I moved to Los Angeles to attend Icon Collective, a music production school where I focused on developing my technical and creative skills. After returning to Livermore, I worked at the Bankhead Theater doing live audio and stage work, which gave me experience working behind the scenes in live production environments.

During the COVID period I moved to Manteca and began working in sales at T-Mobile. That experience helped me develop stronger communication skills and a deeper understanding of sales and customer relationships. At the same time I continued pursuing self education and began building ecommerce businesses and learning more about online entrepreneurship.

Today I live in San Diego and my main focus is building and growing my ecommerce business, Prime Wine Coolers. It’s where I’m putting most of my energy, experimenting with strategies, and honing my entrepreneurial skills.

San Diego has also been a great place to stay active and surround myself with people who inspire me. I spend time riding bikes around the city and learning new skills from friends. Recently, I’ve been diving into cooking and real estate with guidance from friends who have expertise in those areas, which has been both fun and educational.

Being fully immersed in Prime Wine Coolers, while continuing to explore new interests and learn from those around me, keeps me motivated and constantly pushing myself to grow.

Building This Website

This website is a personal project and experiment in learning to code while exploring how AI can be used as a tool to build and create faster. Instead of relying entirely on templates or no-code builders, I wanted to understand how websites actually work and practice writing and modifying the code myself.

Throughout the process, I’ve been learning the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and web design while using AI as a tool to help troubleshoot problems, generate ideas, and speed up development. Rather than replacing the learning process, AI has acted more like a collaborator that helps me iterate and improve more quickly.

This project is part portfolio, part learning experience. It allows me to showcase my work in music and other projects while also documenting my progress in coding and technology. As I continue learning, the website will evolve with new features, design improvements, and additional projects.

Music

I've been producing music for over 10 years, primarily focusing on lo-fi hip-hop and electronic genres. Over the years I've developed a strong passion for sound design, composition, and the technical side of music production. I'm currently focused on continuing to improve my mixing, sound design, and overall production quality.

In 2017, I graduated from Icon Collective College of Music in Burbank, California with a certificate in music production. My time there helped deepen my understanding of music theory, arrangement, and modern production techniques.

From 2018 to 2020, I worked in live audio at Bankhead Theater in Livermore, California, where I gained hands-on experience working with professional sound systems and live performances.

During that time, I also co-ran Windmill Studio in Livermore with Brandon Rios, where we worked with local artists on recording, production, and mixing projects.
